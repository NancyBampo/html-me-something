All I Want For Christmas..

Christmas is a commercialized holiday and I want to spend some time telling you why I love it despite all of that.

Christmas in my opinion,is great if you decide to make it about the things you
you appreciate and cherish in life. When everything about Christmas is about buying
and getting and selling and loss, it becomes very easy to despise it.
Even though I have lost and had a rough year, I still am very greatful for the things I gained and have.
This reason is why I live Christmas.

No more chitter chatter. Let's delve into what I want for Christmas this year.

Here's a list of what I want for Christmas this year :)

  • I want more laughter this Christmas. It sounds cheesy but laughter
    was not my friend this year, so i want more of it.
  • On a lighter note; I want to party. I want to throw a big party on Christmas day.
  • I want to finally pull a successful prank on my husband
  • I want a puppy(probably far fetched, but hey a girl can dream)
  • Lastly, I want more food. hahaha

Tell me what you want for Christmas, or you can tell me why you hate it.
